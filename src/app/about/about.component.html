We all grew up playing video games during the 80's and 90's. And one thing that raised our curiousity levels was the way the characters were made.

PIXELS

This is a small and fun project that tries to do the same in modern times. Full of colors and reminiscent of the comic book aesthetics, see your favorite characters pixelated in all their glory.

The characters belong to Marvel, DC and my personal favorite, Naruto anime series.

So, just click on your favorite characters and jump on this adventure bandwagon.
